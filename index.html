About

I am a third-year PhD student at Dartmouth College, advised by Prof. Bo Zhu. During my PhD study, I have spent wonderful times interning at the Advanced Innovation Center for Future Visual Entertainment (AICFVE) in Beijing Film Academy, and Toyota Research Institute of North America. Before that, I received my master’s degree in Aeronautics&Astronautics from Stanford University in 2017, and my bachelor’s degree in Aerospace Engineering from Beihang University in 2015.

My research interests include physical simulation and numerical optimization, especially in accurate and efficient modeling of solids and fluids as well as their interactions.
